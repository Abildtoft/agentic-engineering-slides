# Why I think Codex will win

Anthropic Is Betting on the Wrong Horse

So here is my final spiciest hot take.

Anthropic built Claude Code for engineers. Collaboration. Dialogue. Humans in the loop. That's a beautiful vision if you think the future is "talented engineers with really smart assistants".

But I don't think that's the future.

The future is millions of non-technical people building software with AI. People who don't care about the craft. People who just want the result.

And Codex is built for them.
